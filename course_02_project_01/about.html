About This Site

This website is a task tracking app. Or rather, I should say it's a demo mock-up of a task-tracking app. A task can be anything, from something simple like "take out the trash" to something more complicated, like "save the world from some bad guys."

Eventually, this can be developed into a more complicated hierarchy (multiple task lists, multiple users, etc.) for project tracking. But for now, we stick to a simple to-do list. One of the tasks is clearly more complicated than the others. :-)
